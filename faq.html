Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our painting services on Oahu.

How do I get a painting estimate?+
Simply use our online booking form or call us at (808) 555-0105. We'll schedule a free in-home visit within 2-3 business days. After the walkthrough, you'll have a detailed written estimate within 24-48 hours.
How long does an exterior paint job last in Hawaii?+
With proper prep and premium tropical-grade paints, a professionally done exterior paint job in Hawaii should last 7-10 years. Factors like sun exposure, proximity to the ocean, and tree coverage all affect longevity.
Do you use paints specifically made for Hawaii's climate?+
Yes — we exclusively use paints with built-in mildewcide, UV-resistant pigments, and 100% acrylic formulations rated for tropical climates. We stock Sherwin-Williams Duration, Benjamin Moore Aura, and other premium climate-appropriate lines.
Do you offer a workmanship guarantee?+
All our exterior projects are backed by a 3-year workmanship guarantee. If anything peels, blisters, or fails within 3 years due to application issues (not physical damage), we'll come back and fix it at no charge.
How long will my painting project take?+
A typical single-family exterior takes 3-5 days including prep, painting, and touch-ups. Interior projects depend on room count — most 3-bedroom homes take 2-3 days. We'll give you a detailed timeline in your estimate.
Do I need to be home during the project?+
You don't need to be home during the work, but we ask that you're available for the start of the first day (to review the project plan) and for the final walkthrough when we confirm everything meets your expectations.
Can you help me choose my paint colors?+
Absolutely. Color consultation is included free with every painting project. Our certified color consultants will bring paint fans to your home, assess lighting and surroundings, and help you create a beautiful, cohesive palette.
Do you handle repairs before painting?+
Yes. We repair minor wood rot, replace failed caulk, fill cracks in stucco and concrete block, and address any surface issues found during prep. Significant structural repairs beyond normal prep scope are quoted separately.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Yes. Aloha Color Painting Co. is a licensed Hawaii painting contractor and carries full general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We're happy to provide our license number and insurance certificates upon request.
What areas of Oahu do you serve?+
We serve all of Oahu, including Pearl City, Honolulu, Aiea, Mililani, Waipahu, Ewa Beach, Kaneohe, Kailua, and surrounding areas. We are based in Pearl City and typically serve projects within a 40-mile radius.

Still have questions? We're happy to help.
