Our Story

A Pearl City family painting Hawaii's homes and hearts since 2009.

Founded in Pearl City

Born & Raised on Oahu

Aloha Color Painting Co. was founded in 2009 by Keola Makoa, a third-generation Pearl City resident who grew up watching his father paint homes across Oahu. After working for a mainland painting franchise for several years, Keola decided it was time to build something local — a company that truly understood Hawaii's climate, its people, and its aesthetic.

Starting with a single van, two brushes, and a phone full of family referrals, Keola grew Aloha Color into a 12-person crew that has now painted over 1,400 homes across Oahu. Every painter on staff is a Hawaii resident, licensed, and trained specifically for tropical painting conditions.

We believe a fresh coat of paint does more than improve curb appeal — it protects your biggest investment, brings pride to your neighborhood, and adds real value to your property.
